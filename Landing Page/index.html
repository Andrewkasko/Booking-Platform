E-Healthcare

E-Healthcare connects patients to available doctors online
so you can consult and discuss your medical problems without stepping outside your doorstep

  • How does it work?

1. Check who's available?

You will be presented with the doctors available based on your location preferences, their online status and the specialty you require

2. Request to have a chat

You can simply request to conect with the doctor, this can be immediate if the doctor is available.

3. Consultation begins

You will then be presented with a variety of tools assisting you to communicate with your doctor.

Features

Request face to face consultation

Online isn't good enough? Simple request to see your doctor at a time that he is available. The phone number and location will be provided to help..

Book an urgent appointment

Dont have time and need to be seen urgently? Simply request to see a doctor making sure to select the red 'urgently' button that is distinguishable from a regular consultation booking.

Receive prescription online

You dont need to go see your doctor for a 2 minute prescription. The doctor will simply send you a downloadable file that contains the prescription. Just download it and take it to a pharmacy.

Communication tools

Just like social media, you will easily be able to send photos, voice recordings or even take a picture.
